Maintaining a proper record of the patient is quite important for the management of a hospital.
In earlier days the in formations regarding patents were recorded in registers, which occupied too much to space for a small amount of data, alteration and deletion was not possible and records were perilous.
But nowadays advanced computer programs are been used for doing the same in a much more efficient way, 
Now data of thousands of patients can be recorded in just a DVD, in which alteration and deletion can be easily performed and most importantly they are secure from getting disintegrated and form malicious activities.
Such advanced record management system can be easily programmed in C++ language.
